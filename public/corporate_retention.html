Corporate Retention

DeepView has the unique capability to record and archive all conversations on corporate social media channels such as LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter, all in one platform. Meet ever-changing global compliance and regulatory standards, such as MiFID II, SEC, FCA, FINRA, OCC, without affecting your ability to communicate with customers and influencers on their preferred social media channels. The DeepView corporate retention solution is slick, simple and easy to use. Simplicity is at the heart of our solution.
  • Monitor, review and record video, photo and text across private chats and central pages
  • Capture metadata for compliance with global regulation
  • Search archived records and produce reports in minutes
  • Store original posts and track changes to conversations over time – including edited, deleted and hidden content
