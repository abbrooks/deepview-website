About Us

DeepView is the world’s first image recognition machine learning tool for digital compliance and data breaches. Used by some of the world’s largest global banks and regulators already, including Federal Reserve Bank of NY, European Central Bank as well as JP Morgan, Bank of America and Barclays, the SaaS licence is being bought by Chief Compliance Officers wanting a holistic solution to data and compliance risk, that employees pose on their personal social media. The machine learning algorithm DeepView uses has been built over two years, based on the global regulatory laws including the SEC Safeguards Rule, the FCA’s Senior Managers Regime and hundreds of relevant others, as they apply to online use. It is why the regulators feel confident enough in the licence to use DeepView too. The software being built will also be offered to employees to help notify staff when they post an image which risks confidential information around their own personal identity.
